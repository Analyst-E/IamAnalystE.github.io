Overview

Led a ₦10 billion (~$6.25M USD) reforestation project in the Ehor Forest Reserve, end-to-end — from field operations to carbon-credit-grade documentation — as Sustainability Lead and Project Manager at Teasoo Consulting.

Scope & responsibilities

  • Directly and indirectly managed a workforce of 94 staff responsible for nursery raising, planting, and logistics.
  • Owned compliance with the Verra VM0047 methodology for carbon credit generation across all field and documentation workstreams.
  • Designed the operational document suite from scratch: Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), planting logs, and field tracking dashboards.
  • Audited and aligned vendor practices to ensure delivery matched the VM0047 methodology requirements.
  • Reviewed and ensured compliance of core regulatory documents — the Project Design Document (PDD) and Environmental and Social Impact Assessment (ESIA) — against Verra's standards.

Why it mattered

Carbon credit projects live or die on documentation rigor and field traceability. Building the SOPs, logs, and dashboards myself meant the program had a single source of truth that field staff, vendors, and auditors could all work from — directly protecting the project's carbon credit eligibility.

Overview

Managed a Health and Social Impact Assessment (HSIA) for a major oil and gas company across 14 communities in Delta State, conducted in line with Nigeria's Petroleum Industry Act (PIA), International Finance Corporation (IFC) standards, World Bank requirements, and Nigerian environmental regulatory bodies.

Team leadership

  • Led an 11-person multidisciplinary team: 2 professors, 2 PhD holders, an HSE expert, two social impact experts, and 6 field staff — coordinating schedules, resources, and deliverables across all of them.

Technical contribution (Social Science Expert)

  • Designed and led analysis of the household survey instrument.
  • Ran and analyzed focus group discussions (FGDs) across the 14 communities.
  • Aggregated quantitative and qualitative findings into a 160-page assessment report.

Follow-on build: Social Performance Platform

Also project managed the build of a social performance tracking platform for the client, enabling them to:

  • Track community commitments and requests against fulfillment status.
  • Identify which communities received the most investment over time.
  • Track which contractors and staff were hired from which communities — supporting local content accountability.
