My technical writing skills complement my analytical prowess, allowing me to communicate intricate findings effectively. My expertise extends beyond traditional analytics; I am proficient in qualitative analysis with tools like Delve and Nvivo, enabling me to handle diverse data types. Whether it’s through Power BI dashboards or SQL data manipulation, my goal is to deliver clarity and drive results. My passion for data is matched by my commitment to excellence in technical writing, ensuring that every dataset tells a story and every insight drives progress.
